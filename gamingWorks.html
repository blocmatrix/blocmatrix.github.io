Innovating the Regulated Gaming industry with the power of blockchain: Eﬃciency, Security, Scalability, speed. The Challenge

Individual player information on multiple website gaming websites leading to security and privacy issues. Hassle of multiple registration on diﬀerent gaming websites. Lack of eﬃcient ecosystem for regulators, operators, and players to manage their Compliance

Need of the hour

Before any project begins we highlight goals, and communicate what our clients deem as must-haves/non-negotiables.

Introduce the power of Blockchain to the industry Create a digital currency for the regulated gaming market and with the ability to transact fast, seamless with security being the highest priority

Have Digital Wallet for all platforms Desktop/Mobile (IOS/Android) a proprietary application with KYC and AML for the regulated market. Build a secure and scalable solution capable of taking on load of the data inﬂux.

The Solution

Built a blockchain on the lines of XRP technology and create the digital currency. Due to the requirement of the application for the regulated market, we developed a Digital Wallet called the Bankroll Manager with Know your customer ( KYC) & Anti-money laundering(AML) features.

Created back oﬃce using Artiﬁcial Intelligence to generate User Behaviour & Transaction Reports to make it future proof.

The Execution

Our value enhancing design strength comes from the above preliminary research, and with the right answers to the questions we ask– we were able to deliver to our client a custom application that moves fluidly, works assumptively, and is built thoughtfully with the standards listed.
